ABOUT ME

A bit about who I am

I'm a multidisciplinary creator who moves between code editors and sketchbooks with equal comfort. My work lives at the crossroads of technology and aesthetics — building digital experiences that feel as considered as a well-set page of type.

When I'm not pushing pixels or debugging state machines, you'll find me sketching portraits, curating playlists that tell stories, or getting lost in open-world games that feel like living inside a painting.

I believe the best work comes from genuine curiosity, quiet persistence, and a healthy disregard for the phrase "that's how it's always been done."
